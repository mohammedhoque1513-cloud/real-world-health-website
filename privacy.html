Privacy Policy

How we handle information shared through this website.

This is a prototype privacy policy written for presentation purposes. In a live deployment, legal review and jurisdiction-specific updates would be required.

Overview

Real World Health is committed to handling personal information responsibly. This website is designed to share information about our services, publications, and ways to get in touch.

Where personal information is collected, we aim to keep it proportionate, limited to what is necessary, and used for clear business purposes such as responding to enquiries.

Information we collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect information such as your name, email address, organisation, job title, and the content of messages submitted through the contact form.

We may also collect basic technical information about site usage, device type, and browser activity to improve performance and understand how the site is used.

How we use information

We use personal information to respond to enquiries, improve our communications, understand demand for our services, and maintain the effective operation of the website.

We do not use contact data for unrelated purposes and aim to retain it only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was provided.

Information sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers or professional advisors where this is necessary to support business operations, legal obligations, or site management.

Where third parties are involved, we expect them to handle information securely and only for agreed purposes.

Your rights

Depending on the laws that apply to you, you may have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, objection, or restriction of processing in relation to your personal information.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us and we will respond in line with applicable requirements.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how information is handled, please email hello@realworldhealth.com.
